## On Fire
*Your character or their clothing is currently burning.* 
* Whenever you act, you suffer 2 [[Injury#Heat]] damage, if this causes [[Injury#Injury Damage]], you take an additional 2 [[Injury#Heat]] damage the next time this occurs.
* Whenever you would perform an [[Terminology#Action]] [[Endurance]](0), if you fail you may only perform actions to remove this effect.

### Removing
{: .no_toc }
* To put yourself out is an [[Application]]. If you succeed, you are no longer on fire.
