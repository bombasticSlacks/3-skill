## Overwatch
*This creature is ready to shoot at a moments notice.*

* When: An enemy enters your [Eye Line](Game/Core/Terminology#Eye%20Line) and you are not already in [Combat](Game/Core/Combat).
* Do: An [Attack](Game/Core/Terminology#Attack) [Action](Game/Core/Terminology#Action) against them.
