## Addictive
*This item requires constant use or a user will suffer withdrawal.*
* After using this, every day you must either:
	* Use a similar [[Consumable]] again.
	* [[Endurance]].
	* Gain 1 [[Stress]].
* This continues until [[Telling-The-Story#Downtime]].
