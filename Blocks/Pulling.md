### Pulling
*This weapon pulls its target closer to you.*
* After a [[Terminology#Successful Attack]] until your [[Terminology#Opponent]] next performs an [[Terminology#Action]] you may as a [[Terminology#Free Action]] move your [[Terminology#Opponent]] within [[Movement#Reach]] of you and cause them to go [[Prone]].
